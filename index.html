About Me

Hi there! I'm Adeshina Sukura, from Nigeria, a passionate professional with a diverse background in design and frontend development. With a keen eye for detail and a love for problem-solving, I've crafted a career that blends creativity with technical expertise.

I have extensive experience in graphic design, UI/UX design, and web design. Transitioning from design, and honed my skills in frontend development, specializing in HTML, CSS, Bootstrap and JavaScript. I pride myself on creating responsive and dynamic websites that elevate user experiences. I've also expanded my skill set to include backend development, mastering languages like Python and frameworks like Django and Flask. This enables me to build robust and scalable web applications from end to end.

Outside of work, I enjoy pursuing various hobbies that fuel my creativity and passion for technology like photography, coding challenges and partaking in hosting kids coding/graphics classes. Looking ahead, I'm excited to continue my journey in frontend development, leveraging my backend skills to create innovative solutions that make a positive impact. I'm committed to lifelong learning and staying at the forefront of technology trends, always striving to push the boundaries of what's possible in the digital realm.
